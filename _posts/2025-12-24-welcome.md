We're excited to announce the launch of the official etive.io website! This new platform serves as the central hub for the asimov ecosystem, bringing together documentation, tutorials, and resources for gravitational-wave analysis.

## What is asimov?

asimov is a framework for managing and orchestrating gravitational-wave parameter estimation analyses. It provides a unified interface to multiple analysis pipelines, making it easier to run reproducible analyses across different codes.

## What's on This Website?

The new website includes:

- **Tutorials**: Step-by-step guides to help you get started with asimov and its ecosystem
- **Documentation**: Links to comprehensive documentation for all packages
- **Blog**: Updates, announcements, and insights from the asimov team
- **Releases**: Track the latest releases across all ecosystem packages
- **Citing**: Information on how to properly cite asimov in your research
- **Contributing**: Guidelines for contributing to the project

## Get Involved

asimov is an open-source project, and we welcome contributions from the community. Whether you're fixing bugs, adding features, improving documentation, or sharing your experiences, your contributions help make gravitational-wave analysis better for everyone.

Visit our [Contributing page](/contributing) to learn how you can get involved!

## Stay Updated

Follow us on [GitHub](https://github.com/etive-io) to stay up-to-date with the latest developments in the asimov ecosystem. We'll be posting regular updates here on the blog about new features, releases, and community highlights.

Thank you for being part of the asimov community!
